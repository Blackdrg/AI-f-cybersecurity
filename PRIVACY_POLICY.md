# Privacy Policy - LEVI-AI

**Last Updated**: April 23, 2026

## 1. Introduction
LEVI-AI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals whose biometric data is processed by our Face Recognition System. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect biometric data.

## 2. Data Collection
We collect the following biometric data:
- Face Embeddings (512-dimensional vectors)
- Optional: Voice and Gait signatures
- Consent records

**We do NOT store raw images unless explicitly configured for forensic auditing by the enterprise client.**

## 3. Purpose of Processing
Biometric data is processed solely for:
- Identity verification and access control.
- Attendance monitoring.
- Security and threat detection.

## 4. Legal Basis (GDPR/CCPA)
We process biometric data based on **Explicit Consent**. No individual is enrolled without a signed consent record stored in our Consent Vault.

## 5. Data Retention
Biometric data is retained only as long as the individual is an active member of the client's organization or until consent is revoked.

## 6. Your Rights
Under GDPR, you have the following rights:
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your biometric data (Data Portability).
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your biometric data (Right to be Forgotten).
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Revoke consent at any time.

## 7. Contact
For Data Subject Access Requests (DSAR), please contact: `privacy@levi-ai.com`
